A World Away 🌌

Hunting for Exoplanets with Artificial Intelligence

About the Project

Our project uses Artificial Intelligence to identify exoplanets from NASA’s Kepler and TESS data. By training AI models to detect transit signals, we accelerate the search for worlds beyond our solar system.

How It Works

  • 🛰️ Collects NASA Kepler Light Curve data
  • 🤖 Processes signals using AI (TensorFlow & Python)
  • 🔭 Identifies possible exoplanets with confidence scores

Tools & Data Sources

- NASA Exoplanet Archive - Kepler & TESS datasets - Python, TensorFlow, Pandas, Matplotlib
